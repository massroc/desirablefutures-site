## Our Services
Our consultancy offers tailored services to support change for organisations. From education and training to design and implementation we can help.

Education and Training
We've given talks at conferences all over the country and run scores of training courses. If you want to learn more about autonomous teams and scale we can teach you. If you'd like the tools to do it for yourself we can help.

Organisational Diagnosis
If you'd like to know where you are on your journey and where you can focus your efforts we have a comprehensive set of tools to give you evidence-based insight. Leverage our deep knowledge to give yourself a path forward.

Facilitation
We are master facilitators who have facilitated events for thousands of people over the years. Whether it is a board offsite, participative design or team kick-offs we have lots of engaging formats that create insight and outcomes.

Consulting
If you need on-going assistance we can help. In particular we support leaders who are leading organisational change whether it's designing, implementing, or course-correction. We can help you can avoid common mistakes and set yourself up for success.
