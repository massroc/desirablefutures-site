## Intro
Sometimes you need to step back from the day-to-day and create space for your people to think together. Whether you're planning strategy, solving complex problems, or designing how you want to work together, we help you run sessions that actually get results.

We bring proven methods like Search Conference and participative work design to help your team have productive conversations and make decisions that stick. We've facilitated everything from executive off-sites and board retreats to large multi-team events and team kick-offs. The best part? You get immediate value without needing to commit to a long-term contract.

## Services

### Executive and Board Strategy Sessions
When your leadership team needs to tackle big decisions or chart future direction, we design and facilitate sessions that get everyone thinking clearly and working together. We help you move beyond endless discussions to actual alignment on what matters most. Your team leaves with clarity on priorities and concrete next steps that everyone understands and supports.

### Team Kick-offs and Planning Sessions
Starting a new project or forming a new team? We help you get off on the right foot. Using proven techniques, we facilitate sessions where your team can get to know each other, understand what they're trying to achieve together, and figure out how they want to work. Your team builds relationships and creates their own ways of working that set them up for success.

### Large Group Events That Actually Work
Multi-team workshops don't have to be painful. We use participative design methods to help large groups of people have meaningful conversations and reach real decisions together. Whether you're bringing together different departments, planning a major change, or solving problems that affect multiple teams, we create structured processes where everyone's voice is heard and good ideas surface.

### Problem-Solving Workshops for Complex Issues
Some problems can't be solved by one person or department alone. We facilitate collaborative sessions using Search Conference methods to help diverse groups understand complex challenges together and design solutions that actually work. Your team gets clarity on the real issues and develops approaches that everyone can get behind because they helped create them.
