Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This privacy policy describes how Desirable Futures ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and handles your personal information when you use our website at www.desirablefutures.group.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you submit a contact form. This may include your name, email address, and the content of your message.

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics. We do not use third-party advertising trackers.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Contact form

Messages submitted through our contact form are processed by Formspree (formspree.io). Please refer to Formspree's privacy policy for details of how they handle submitted data.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at ross@desirablefutures.group.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at ross@desirablefutures.group.
