New White Paper: Industry 5.0 – A Human-Centric Approach to the Engagement Crisis

Desirable Futures  ·  15 Sep 2024

We are delighted to announce the release of our latest white paper, Industry 5.0: A Human-Centric Approach to the Engagement Crisis.

As organisations increasingly adopt new technologies, many face a deeper challenge—employee disengagement. This white paper explores how Industry 5.0 shifts the focus from automation to human augmentation, where AI and advanced technologies free up humans for more meaningful and creative work. Rather than replacing humans, Industry 5.0 empowers employees to unleash their creativity and engage in high-value tasks, fostering greater innovation, sustainability, and resilience.

Learn how leading companies like Haier and Buurtzorg are pioneering this transformation and discover practical steps to integrate these principles into your organisation.
