The Future of Work is Human — Webinar

Desirable Futures  ·  1 Sep 2024

AI, robotics, and blockchain are driving significant changes in industries, while hybrid and remote work are reshaping how we operate. As organisations navigate these shifts, many are struggling with outdated practices that lead to low engagement and high turnover risks. It's the human element that often gets the least attention but holds the highest importance.

The future is here, and the future of work is human-centric. Learn how pioneering organisations have adopted a human-centric approach first and used technology to support them.
